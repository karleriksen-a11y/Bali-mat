🦠 Bali Belly Guide — What To Watch Out For
💧Ice cubes — Often made with tap water. Stick to bottled drinks or ask for "no ice".
🥗Raw salads & fruit — Washed with tap water. Ask if it's peeled/cooked.
🍖Buffet food — Sitting out for hours. High-risk, especially in heat.
🐟Raw fish / sushi — Only at top-end, well-reviewed places.
🌮Warungs / street food — Higher risk but often fine. Check turnover.
🧴If you get it — Electrolytes, rest, Imodium. Go to BIMC if fever/blood.
